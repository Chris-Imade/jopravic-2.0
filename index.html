Our Story

From a Small Office in Benin City to National Policy Change

In just five years, JOPRAVIC CDI has gone from a local idea to a nationally recognised force — working across 6 states to protect children, strengthen institutions, and give communities the tools to build their own futures.

Data That Drives Policy

We don't guess. Every programme is measured, documented, and used to shape real government policy.

Communities Lead the Way

We design every programme with — not for — the people we serve. That's how change lasts.

  • Co-authored Nigeria's national victim compensation guideline with NAPTIP & IOM
  • Unlocked attention on ₦2.60 Billion in unaccessed UBEC education funds
  • Launched 3 survivor-led businesses and championed the 2km school access standard

Why JOPRAVIC CDI

Built on Evidence. Driven by Purpose.

We don't do charity for its own sake. Every programme we run is backed by data, shaped by community input, and designed to create change that outlasts our involvement. Here's what guides us.

  • Strengthen governance systems that protect the most vulnerable
  • Drive evidence-based advocacy that shapes national policy
  • Empower communities to lead their own development
  • A Nigeria where every child is safe, educated, and thriving
  • Institutions that are transparent, accountable, and effective
  • Communities that sustain their own progress for generations
  • Every programme is measured, documented, and independently verified
  • Recognised by NAPTIP, IOM, UBEC, and state governments
